    Welcome to Atlantica Online Reborn

    Atlantica Online is a turn-based tactical MMORPG in which the player primarily assumes the role of a descendant of the Atlantean people. The primary goal of the game is to search for your ancestral home to save the world from the effects of Oriharukon, a substance created by the ancient Atlanteans. The player's journey spans the globe and transcends time in search of clues that will lead to the lost civilization. The player battles men, monsters, and machines that have been corrupted by the Oriharukon's Influence

    Welcome to Lost Saga

    Lost Saga is a free-to-play online game of the 3D Casual Fighting type. The game was developed by IO Entertainment from South Korea. Lost Saga officially released their services in the United States on November 17, 2009, through developer OGPlanet, while Z8Games became the permanent host since December 18, 2013.

    Welcome to Dragonest

    Dragon Nest is a free-to-play fantasy MMORPG developed by Eyedentity. The game uses a manual system so that players have full control over their character's every move. Dragon Nest asks players to upgrade their characters by creating teams and traveling to dungeons.

    Welcome to PointBlank

    Point Blank Is As a result of the widespread conflict with immigrants, the government decided to form a special organization to deal with terrorists. Since the formation of this organization, they began to search for information and the existence of a terrorist organization called Free Rebels. In line with the increasing terrorist threat, the government then sent the best troops ever in the government to come and join and change the name to CT-Force (Counter Terrorist Force).
